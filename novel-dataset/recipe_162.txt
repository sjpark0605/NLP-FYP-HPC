divide the rhubarb and caster sugar between two large jars. make sure the lids are secured firmly in place then give each jar a vigorous shake. pour half of the vodka into each jar and then replace the lids again. store the jars in a cool dark place for at least 2 months. during this time , give the jars a shake 2-3 times a week. after 2 months the vodka can be strained into a litre bottle.
