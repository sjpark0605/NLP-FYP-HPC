place beef in bottom of cooker and season with salt and pepper to taste. add onion , soup , mushrooms , parsley , chives , broth , wine , garlic and worcestershire. add to cooker. cook on low for 7-8 hours. one hour before serving , stir in sour cream. i usually don't add this step if adding the egg noodles because they will thicken the sauce. if using frozen egg noodles , par boil for 10 minutes and add to sauce. cook on low for 1 hr.
