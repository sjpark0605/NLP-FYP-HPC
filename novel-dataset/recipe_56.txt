combine first 4 ingredients in a large bowl. combine eggs and milk. add to dry ingredients , stirring just until moistened. stir in cheese , pepper , corn , and onion. do not overstir. pour oil to depth of 2 inches into a small dutch oven. heat to 375f. carefully drop batter by rounded tablespoons into oil. fry hush puppies a few at a time 3 minutes or until golden brown , turning once. drain on paper towels.
