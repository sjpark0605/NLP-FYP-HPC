preheat oven to 350. cut green peppers in half , discarding seeds. in large sauce pan over high heat cook peppers in boiling salted water for 5 minutes. drain. in a large bowl , blend cream cheese with sour cream and milk until smooth. stir in onions , 1 / 2 teaspoons. salt , dry mustard and garlic powder. stir in sliced hot dogs and cooked macaroni. spoon macaroni mixture into pepper halves. place prepared peppers into a 13x9 baking dish that has been sprayed with cooking spray. cover bake for 25 minutes. remove cover and sprinkle with the shredded cheese. return to oven for 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
