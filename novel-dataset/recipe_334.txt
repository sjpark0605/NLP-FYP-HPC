heat oil in a medium nonstick skillet and sautes the peppers , onion , celery , celery and garlic until the onion is translucent. add mushrooms and saute until mushrooms are brown and the liquid has evaporated. cool to room temperature. spray broiler rack with nonstick cooking spray and preheat broiler. combine mushroom mixture , beef , steak sauce , salt and pepper. form into 4 burgers. broil burgers 3 to 4 inches from heat for 5 to 7 minutes on each side.
