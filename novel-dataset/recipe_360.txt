preheat the oven to approximately 375f and have ready a casserole dish , approximately 8x10 inches , lightly buttered. in a clean saucepan , melt the butter and lightly saut the finely diced onions. add a pinch of cayenne pepper and salt. add the milk and bring to a simmer. add the cornmeal and work briskly with a wooden spoon to prevent any lumps. cook the cornmeal for 6 to 8 minutes , stirring gently over low heat. remove from heat and allow to cool for 15 minutes. next add the cheese , the egg yolks and the corn and stir well so that everything is thoroughly mixed. whisk the egg whites so as they are at a soft peak stage and then incorporate 1 / 3 at a time to a corn mixture. pour everything into the casserole and bake for approximately 20 to 30 minutes. let cool for 5 minutes prior to serving. chef michael bonacini.
