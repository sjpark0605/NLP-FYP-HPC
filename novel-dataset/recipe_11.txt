cook pasta according to package directions. drain. meanwhile , heat a large skillet or saute pan over medium high heat and add the olive oil , shrimp and garlic. cook , stirring frequently until the shrimp just turns opaque , about 2 minutes. add the mushrooms , tomatoes , and artichokes and keep cooking until the mushrooms are softened , about 3 minutes. season to taste with salt and pepper. toss with pasta and garnish with fresh parsley.
