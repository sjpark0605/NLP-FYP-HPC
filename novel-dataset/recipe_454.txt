in a large shallow glass dish , combine the first seven ingredients. add chicken. turn to coat. cover and refrigerate for 3-4 hours. place cracker crumbs in a shallow bowl. roll chicken in crumbs until coated. transfer to a greased 13x9x2 inch baking dish. combine butter and oil. pour over the chicken. bake , uncovered , at 350 for 50-60 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.
