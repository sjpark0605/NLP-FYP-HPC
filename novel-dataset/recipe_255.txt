preheat oven to 375f. unroll both tubes of crescent dough and pat into an ungreased 15x10x1-inch baking pan. seal seams and perforations. bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. cool completely on a wire rack. meanwhile , in a small mixing bowl , beat the cream cheeses , yogurt , mayo , milk , dill and garlic until smooth. spread over crust. sprinkle with carrots , cauliflower , broccoli , green pepper , mushrooms , olives and onion. cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. cut into 24 squares and serve. refrigerate leftovers.
