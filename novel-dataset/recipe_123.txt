preheat oven to 325f prepare a shallow 14x11-inch pan by greasing with cooking spray or butter. in large bowl , thoroughly mix together oats , walnuts , sesame seeds , orange zest , sugar , and salt. in a separate bowl combine the honey and oil. pour over the dry ingredients. thoroughly mix together and spoon into the prepared pan. bake for 30 minutes , turning the pan and stirring every 10 minutes , or until golden brown. remove from oven. mix in the dried cranberries and blueberries , if using , and allow to cool.
