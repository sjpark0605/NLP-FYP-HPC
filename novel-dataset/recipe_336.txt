preheat oven to 375 f degrees. in large bowl , mix saurkraut , onion , parsley , and caraway seed. spoon into 2 qt. baking dish. layer with half the cheese , half the salad dressing , and all of the corned beef. layer remaining salad dressing and cheese. in large skillet , melt butter , add bread cubes , and stir until bread is coated. sprinkle bread over top of casserole. bake 30 minutes until all is hot and bread cubes are browned.
