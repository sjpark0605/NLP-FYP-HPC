preheat oven to 425 degrees. coat a 11 x 13 inch roasting pan with nonstick cooking spray. place califlower , onion rings , and mushrooms in the pan , sprinkle the vegetables with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil , toss to mix well. cover the pan with aluninum foil , bake for 10 minutes. remove foil and bake another 15 to 20 minutes stirring every 5 minutes , or until vegetables are tender and lightly browned , serve hot.
