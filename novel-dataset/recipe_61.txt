bring water and butter to a boil in a 2 quart heavy saucepan over high heat , then reduce heat to moderate add flour all at once and cook , stirring vigorously with a wooden spoon until mixture pulls away from the pan remove pan from heat and add eggs , 1 at a time , stirring vigorously after each addition until smooth preheat oven to 425f. transfer dough to a pastry bag and pipe mounds 1 / 3 inch in diameter about 1 / 2 inch apart on 2 large baking sheets lined with parchment. gently flatten. bake 1 sheet at a time , in middle of oven until golden brown and cooked through , 12-15 minutes.
