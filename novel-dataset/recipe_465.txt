in a large pot of boiling water , cook fettuccine until tender but firm. drain and return to saucepan. meanwhile , in a food processor , process cottage cheese until smooth. add parmesan cheese , milk , egg , nutmeg , and pepper. blend until smooth. pour into saucepan with hot cooked pasta. cook over medium heat for 1 minute , stirring constantly. sprinkle with parsley or basil. serve immediately.
