heat oven to 350f. in large skillet , brown pork chops in oil. place in ungreased 13x9 inch pan. in medium bowl , combine soup , celery , onions , egg , pepper and poultry seasoning. mix well. seperate dough into 10 biscuits. cut each into 8 pieces. stir biscuit pieces into soup mixture. spoon over pork chops. bake for 45 to 55 minutes or until biscuit pieces are golden brown.
