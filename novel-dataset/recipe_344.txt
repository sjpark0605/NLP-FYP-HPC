preheat oven to 350f. in a large bowl combine ground beef , 1 cup sauce , 1 / 2 cup green peppers , 1 / 2 cup oats , 1 / 2 cup cheese , eggs , salt and pepper. in a 13 x 9 baking pan , shape into loaf. top with 1 / 2 cup sauce. bake uncovered for 50 minutes. top with 1 / 2 cup cheese and bake an additional 10 minutes. let stand 10 minutes before serving. remove meat loaf to serving platter and serve with sauce. 377 calories per serving , 26. 8 g fat , 7. 2 g carbohydrates.
