preheat oven to 400. spray 3 9-inch pie pans with cooking spray. on lightly floured surface , roll out one pizza crust and pat into 12x8 rectangle. cut into 24 squares. place one pepperoni slice and one cube of cheese on each dough square. wrap dough around , pressing to seal. place in pans , seamside down. repeat with remaining dough , pepperoni and cheese. mix together oil and italian seasoning. drizzle or brush over rolls. top with grated parmesan. bake at 400 for 16-20 minutes or until golden brown. serve with warmed pizza or marinara sauce. to freeze: flash freeze on a cookie sheet until frozen solid , label and place in a ziplock bag. to reheat: microwave approximately 30 seconds or more to heat through.
