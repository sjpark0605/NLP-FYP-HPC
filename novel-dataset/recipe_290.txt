preheat oven to 180c and spray a baking dish with cooking spray. place fillets into dish and brush with the macadamia oil. in a small bowl combine the crust ingredients and mix to form a thick paste. spread the macadamia crust over the top of the fish fillets. press on evenly using your hands or the back of a soon. bake for approx 10 minutes , or until the fish flakes when tested with a fork. the cooking time will very depending on the thickness of the fish. check for doneness after each few minutes.
