rinse and drain all the canned food and put in a large bowl for now. finely chop the celery and put it in the bowl too. cook the ditalini al dente and put it aside for now , still in the water so it doesn't dry out. put the vegetable broth , oil , and tomato paste into a large saucepan , mix together on high heat. when this completely melds , lower the heat and add the minced garlic , onion powder , black pepper , and basil. stir. add the whole bowl of veggies and blend in well. drain the ditalini and add to the mix. let simmer for 10-15 minutes or until thick and bubbly.
