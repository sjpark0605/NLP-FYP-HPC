in a sauce pan combine soup , milk , butter , chopped onions , and velveeta cheese. stir several times until the cheese is melted. remove from heat and fold in the mayonnaise and sour cream. in a bowl combine ham , potatoes , seasonings , the broccoli or choice of vegetable if adding one , and only 1 / 2 of the sauce. set aside the other 1 / 2 sauce covered with a lid. sprinkle the cheese evenly over the casserole. bake in hot 375 oven uncovered for 20 to 30 minutes or until the cheese topping turns light toasty brown. served with a nice green salad and french bread makes a nice meal.
