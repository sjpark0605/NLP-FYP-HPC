sift together flour , baking powder , baking soda , salt , and 3 tablespoons sugar. combine egg , sour cream , and oil in bowl and pour into dry ingredients all at once. stir partially , then add apples which have been peeled , diced , and sprinkled with nutmeg and 2 tablespoons sugar. stir until all dry ingredients are moist but still lumpy , about 17-25 strokes. spoon batter into greased muffin tins , filling tins only 2 / 3 full. bake at 425 for 20-28 minutes.
