steam or boil asparagus until tender-crisp. spread each side of ham with mustard. lay 3 asparagus spears on each slice , alternating. roll these up and place seam side down in casserole dish. melt butter in med size saucepan. stir in flour slowly , then add milk. stir and cook until boiling. add salt and pepper. stir in cheese until melted , add green onion slices. stir and pour over meat rolls. cover and bake in 350 f oven 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
