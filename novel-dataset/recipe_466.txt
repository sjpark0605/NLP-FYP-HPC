in a large skillet , brown and cook crumbled sausage until no longer pink. drain. remove from skillet. set aside. in the same skillet , melt butter ansd saut onion , celery and green pepper until tender. in an extra large mixing bowl , combine sausage , vegetable mixture , bread stuffing and seasonings. add eggs and broth. toss gently. salt and pepper to taste. spoon into a buttered 3 to 4 quart baking dish. cover with foil and bake at 325 for 60 minutes. uncover and bake 10 minutes longer or until lightly browned. check internal temperature with a meat thermometer , it should read 165f when done.
