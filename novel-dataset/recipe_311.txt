in a large non-stick pot , brown beef lightly and drain off fat. add lentils , barley , water or stock and beef broth. bring to a boil , reduce heat and simmer , covered , until lentils and barley are nearly tender. add remaining ingredients and simmer about 1 to 1-1 / 2 hours. add extra liquid to give desired consistency. salt and pepper generously to taste and sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley.
