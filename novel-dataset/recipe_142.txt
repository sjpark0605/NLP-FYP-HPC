in a medium-size saucepan put the cream cheese and butter , and cook over low heat , stirring frequently , until smooth. remove from heat. crush the ritz crackers and place them in a bowl. add just enough milk to moisten the crackers and blend. in a greased casserole dish layer 1 / 2 broccoli , 1 / 2 chicken , 1 / 2 grated cheeses , 1 / 2 crushed crackers and 1 / 2 cream cheese sauce. repeat the layers. bake in a 350f oven for 35-45 minutes , or until everything is hot and the cheese has melted. this can also be made without the chicken.
