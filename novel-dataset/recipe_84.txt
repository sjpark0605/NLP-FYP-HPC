stir chickpea flour and turmeric into yoghurt. set aside. heat ghee. add chillies , ginger , cinnamon , cumin , curry leaf , peanuts and mustard. stir briefly. add asafetida , stirrring briefly again. lower heat and add yoghurt mixture and salt. cook partially covered on a medium heat for 15 - 20 minutes till soup has thickened. garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with basmati rice.
