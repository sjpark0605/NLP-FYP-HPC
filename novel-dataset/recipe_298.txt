blend 2 cups corn and 1 cup of broth in blender until almost smooth. melt butter in large saucepan over medium-high heat. add next 3 ingredients. saute 5 minutes. add 2 cups of corn , 1 cup broth , and puree from blender. bring to simmer. reduce heat to medium-low , cover , simmer until potatoes are tender , about 10 minutes. mix in cream. season chowder with salt and pepper. ladle into bowls. sprinkle with onions.
