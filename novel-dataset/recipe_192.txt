for the beancurds ---------------. mixed minced pork , some corrianders , mushrooms and cooking wine , salt , pepper , corn flour together. fill the hole on each beancurd with the mixture. put the beancurds into the steamer to steam for 15 minutes. for the gravy --------------------. boil 1 / 2 cup of water in a small saucepan. add oyster sauce , corn flour , pepper and salt to the boiling water and stir well. turn off the gas and add 1 tbsp of cooking wine or sherry and the remaining corriander to the gravy and stir well. sprinkle the gravy over the steamed beancurds and serve.
