cook spaghetti according to package directions. drain , rinse with cold water , and drain well. transfer to large bowl. in medium bowl , whisk together salad dressing , cheese , seeds , salt , paprika , garlic powder , and peppers until well blended. stir in cucumber , onion , and tomatoes. pour over spaghetti and toss lightly to coat evenly. cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours. garnish with parsley.
