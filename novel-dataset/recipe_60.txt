preheat oven to 175 c. grease and flour a 2 litre cake pan. whip eggs with sugar until light and fluffy. add the whipped cream to the egg mixture , mix to combine. in a separate bowl , combine the flour with baking soda and the spices. add dry ingredient mixture alternately with the molasses , mixing well after each addition. finally , add the melted butter , mix to combine. pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake at 175 c for 30 minutes. after the first 30 minutes , lower the oven temperature to 150 c and bake for further 20-30 minutes , or until cake tests done.
