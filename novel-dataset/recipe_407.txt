dissolve sugar and salt in water. add milk , egg and flour and blend well. add the margarine. knead the dough until smooth and no longer sticky. divide dough into 70 g pieces. shape the dough into balls and coat with margarine. cover the dough and leave for about five hours or overnight. gently press the dough with palm , stretching it slightly. hold the dough and stretch to expand the dough piece. repeat this step for about eight times. finally you should get a piece of thin sheet of dough. fold up or coil into a lump. leave the dough for ten minutes. fry on both sides over medium heat until golden brown. serve roti canai with curry.
