preheat oven to 350. prepare squash by washing , poking 2-4 holes into center with sharp knife. bake whole squash in oven for 60-90 minutes , until soft. when squash is done , cut in half , remove seeds and fibers , and scrape flesh from skin into a bowl , mash but do not puree squash. melt butter in a large skillet. add sage leaves and fry until crisp 1-2 minutes. add leek and salt and continue heating 2-5 minutes until leeks are soft and translucent. now add squash to skillet and mix until all butter is incorporated.
