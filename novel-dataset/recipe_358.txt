heat oven to 425 degrees. spray muffins tins. in a bowl combine dry ingredients and blend. melt butter in saucepan. turn off heat and stir in applesauce and milk. beat in egg. stir applesauce mixture into dry ingredients and , when well-blended , stir in raisins and walnuts. bake 15-20 minutes , depending on size of muffin tin. let stand for 5 minutes before removing from tins.
