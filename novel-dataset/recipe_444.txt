spray medium size deep soup pot with non-stick spray. saut onions until clear and softened. add chicken breast and saut until no longer pink. add cumin , garlic powder and taco seasoning packet. start opening cans and start dumping: begin with the broth , beans , refried beans , and add all other ingredients. stir well. bring to a boil then simmer for at least 40 minutes. longer is fine. add extra chicken broth if soup becomes too thick. serve with warm soft torn fat free tortillas or crispy tortilla chips. top with cilantro , sharp cheddar cheese , sour cream or any other taco toppings you like.
