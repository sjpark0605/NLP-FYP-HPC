beat butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. add eggs , one at a time , mixing well after each addition. blend in marmalade and set mixture aside. combine flour , baking powder , baking soda and salt. add dry ingredients to butter mixture alternately with orange juice. stir in nuts. pour into a greased and floured 9x5" loaf pan. bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until toothpick tests clean. cool for 15 minutes before removing from pan.
