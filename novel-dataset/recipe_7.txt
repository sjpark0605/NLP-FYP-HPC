preheat oven to 350. spray 8" square baking pan with cooking spray and set aside. in medium bowl , combine flour , cocoa and salt. mix well. in large bowl , whisk together egg whites , sugar , applesauce , oil and vanilla. stir in flour mixture until just blended. pour batter into prepared pan. bake until just set and a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean , about 20-25 min. place pan on wire rack and cool at least 15 min. cut brownies into squares.
