in a medium bowl , combine cream cheese , monterey jack cheese , jalapeo peppers , garlic and green onions. mix well and season with a little black pepper. fill the wonton wrappers with approximately 1 tbs. cream cheese mixture. fold wrappers into triangles then press with moistened fingers or fork to seal. in a large skillet heat oil over medium-high heat. carefully place 3-4 wontons at a time into the oil and quickly fry , turning once until golden brown. remove to paper towels. serve warm with your favorite salsa / sauce , ranch dressing , or whatever. a good appetizer with margaritas ! ole !.
