pre-heat oven to 375f. mix sugars , sour cream , molasses , egg , margarine , and shortening in a large bowl until smooth. stir in remaining ingredients. do not over mix !. drop by rounded tablespoonful onto an ungreased cookis sheet. place about 2 inches apart. bake time is roughly 10-12 minutes or until the cookies have browned. may be hard to gage by color so bake until almost no indentation remains in center of cookie when touched. cool on wire rack. serve by themselves or dipped in whipped cream !.
