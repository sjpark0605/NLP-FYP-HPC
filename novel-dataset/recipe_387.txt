remove any white membrane from roast. put roast in a shyallow dish. combine wine , onion , and seasonings , pour over roast. cover and marinate in fridge 8 hours , turn occasionally. remove roast from marinade , reserve marinade. brown roast in dutcxh oven. insert meat thermometer , make sure it doesn't touch any fat or bone. add marinade to roast , cover and bake at 350f for 2 hours or until temp reaches 170f. remove and discard bay leaf. remove roast , reserving marinade. combine flour and water , stir until smooth. add flour mix to marinade , cook over med. stir constantly , until thick. serve gravy with roast.
