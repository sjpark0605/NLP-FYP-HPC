preheat oven to 350 degrees. in large bowl , combine the cookie mix , eggs and butter. mix well. melt over hot water , morsels from the mix. set aside. spread half the cookie batter evenly into a lightly greased square baking pan. dollop melted chocolate alternately with remaining batter on top. with a knife , swirl dough with chocolate to marbleize. bake approximately 20 minutes. cool completely. cut into 2" squares.
