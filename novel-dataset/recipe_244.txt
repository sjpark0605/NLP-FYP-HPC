sprinkle a small amount of salt , pepper , cumin , & paprika on the chicken. pour 1 1 / 2 cups of water into the pressure cooker. add the olive oil , tomato paste , garlic , & the rest of the spices into the pot. place the chicken into the cooker & add the onions & carrot. cook for 10 minutes , after it begins to hiss. remove chicken & place on a plate. put the potatoes & 1 1 / 2 cups of water into the pot & cook for 4 more minutes , after it begins to hiss. while the potatoes are cooking break the chicken into small pieces. once the potatoes are done add the chicken back into the pan & stir. pour into a serving dish & serve with warm bread.
