dressing: combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl. in a food processor , process ginger , garlic , and green onions until chopped fine. whisk into soy mixture. cook pasta according to package directions. drain , rinse under cold water and set aside. heat snow peas and 1 / 2 teaspoon salt in 1 inch water in medium saucepan over high heat until snow peas are bright green , 20 seconds. remove from heat. drain and rinse under cold water. julienne. add snow peas and pasta to dressing with remaining ingredients. toss to coat.
