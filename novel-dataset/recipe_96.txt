halve the potatoes and add to a large pot. cover with water and bring to a boil over high heat. reduce the heat to medium-low and cook the potatoes for 10 minutes while you trim and chop broccoli into 1 / 2-inch dice. add the broccoli to the potatoes and cook for 5 minutes more. drain and return to the hot pot to dry. stir in the milk , butter and cheese and mash until the cheese melts into potatoes and broccoli. season with salt and pepper , to taste.
