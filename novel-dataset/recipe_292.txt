turn the broiler on. lay out the bread or cut the rolls in half lengthwise. spread 2 tbsp cream cheese on each slice / half. thinly spread half a tsp of garlic on each piece. top with cheddar cheese. broil until cheese is bubbling and bread is crisp - depending on the heat of your broiler and how crisp you like it , 2-5 minutes. remove , let cool slightly and enjoy !.
