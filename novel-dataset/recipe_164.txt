spray a short burst of non-stick cooking spray in a large plastic bag. add all ingredients except lemon and toss with seasonings to coat. prepare 2 18 squares of heavy duty foil. to make one pocket , put 1 chicken breast on the foil square and pile 1 / 2 of the veggies around and on top , topping with a lemon slice. repeat with the remaining foil and ingredients. fold and seal foil around the ingredients leaving enough room for steaming. grill over medium heat for 25-30 minutes. careful of steam when opening !.
