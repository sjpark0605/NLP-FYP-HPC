cook lasagna noodles in boiling water until tender. drain and set aside. combine tomato sauce and chopped onions , peppers and mushrooms. mix in separate bowl cottage cheese , eggs and parmesan cheese. preheat oven to 350 degrees. in a 8" x 8" casserole layer half the noodles , the cottage cheese mixture and grated mozzarella cheese. top with tomato sauce mix and the rest of the noodles. sprinkle with parmesan cheese. bake for 25 minutes.
