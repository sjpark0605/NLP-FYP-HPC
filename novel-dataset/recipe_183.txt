mix flour , baking powder and salt in food processor bowl. add butter and pulse til small pea size. don't over process. bisquits - preheat to 400. 1 cup mix and 1 / 4 cup water or milk. stir w / fork til moist ball forms. knead on floured surface 8-10 ties. roll out and cut biscuits. bake 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned. if milk used bake at 350 instead and increase time by 4 minutes. drop biscuit - use a bit more water for a softer dough and drop onto silpat.
