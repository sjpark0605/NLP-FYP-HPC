sprinkle both sides of the steak with the oregano , salt and pepper. heat a large nonstick skillet over medium high. when the pan is very hot , add the steak. cook the steak for 4 minutes , or until it lifts easily off the pan and is browned on the underside. turn the steak and cook another 4 minutes. add the vermouth and tomatoes to the pan , cover , reduce heat to medium , and simmer 25 minutes. turn heat up to medium high and cook another 5 minutes , or until the liquid in the pan has evaporated. transfer the meat to a carving board , and let sit 5 minutes before cutting on the diagonal into very thin strips. serve the steak strips fanned out on the plate , and spoon some of the tomato from the pan over the meat.
