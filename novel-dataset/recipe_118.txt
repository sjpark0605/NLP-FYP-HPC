dough: add first 8 ingredients to bread maker in order listed , select dough cycle. at end of dough cycle , once dough has risen , dump dough onto floured board , knead , divide into 2 equal parts and let rest 10 minutes. spread dough on each of two greased pizza pans. this dough also makes excellent calzones , and also great cinnamon rolls -- or so they say !. pizza:. add pizza sauce , toppings of your choice , and cheeses. bake in preheated 400 f oven 15-20 minutes. remove from oven and let sit for five minutes before cutting.
