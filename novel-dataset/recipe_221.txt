preheat oven to 350. in large bowl , beat milk , peanut butter , egg and vanilla extract until smooth. add biscuit mix. combine. chill dough for 1 hour. roll chilled balls into 1-inch balls. space balls 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet. bake 6-8 minutes-or light browned. right away , top each center of cookies with a kiss. let cool.
