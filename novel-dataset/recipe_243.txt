place pancake mix in a bowl. combine the eggs , milk , oil and maple flavoring. add to the pancake mix and stir , mixing well. pour into a greased 14" pizza pan. sprinkle with the granola and pecans. bake in oven at 425f. for 10-12 minutes , or until a toothpick comes out clean. cut into wedges. serve with syrup , or honey , if you like. enjoy !.
