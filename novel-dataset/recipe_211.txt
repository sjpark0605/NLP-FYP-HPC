preheat oven to 350 degrees. melt butter in bottom of 9" baking dish sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. make cake per package directions. set aside. sprinkle brown sugar in bottom of baking pan. place diced rhubarb on top of brown sugar. pour cake batter over rhubarb. bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes. serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream. if using frozen , just thaw first but do not squeeze water out.
