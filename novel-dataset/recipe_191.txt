set oven to 350 degrees. for the topping: combine all ingredients. set aside. to make the cake: in a large bowl , combine the graham cracker crumbs , flour , brown sugar , baking powder , cinnamon and salt. add in the butter or margarine and milk. beat at high speed for 2 minutes. add in eggs. beat for another 2 minutes more. spread batter in a greased 9-inch square baking pan. sprinkle the chopped apples on top of the batter. then sprinkle the crumb topping evenly over the apples. bake for 35-40 minutes.
