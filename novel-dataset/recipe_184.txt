preheat oven to 400f. whisk together olive oil , balsamic vinegar , and roasted garlic paste until smooth , adding more oil if it is too thick. bend asparagus gently until it breaks at a natural point and discard ends. toss asparagus pieces with roasted garlic mixture until coated and lay in a single layer on a baking sheet. season with salt and pepper. sprinkle asparagus with parmesan cheese. roast at 400f for 12 minutes or until done. sprinkle with snipped chives.
