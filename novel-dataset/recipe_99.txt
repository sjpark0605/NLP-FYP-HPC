wash the squash , cut it in half , and scoop out the seeds. place it , cut side down , in a dish with about 1 / 2 an inch of water and microwave it for about 10 minutes. let it cool in the refrigerator. peel the apple , core it , and chop it up into a blender. scoop out the squash from the skin and place it into the blender. add the apple juice and the ginger and puree. pour and serve.
