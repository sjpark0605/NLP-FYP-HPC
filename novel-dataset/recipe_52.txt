flatten cutlets to 1 / 4-inch thickness then rub with basil , thyme , salt and pepper. heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. add pork and cook 2 minutes per side. remove to a plate and loosely cover with foil to keep warm. add lemon juice and capers to skillet. bring to boil and cook until slightly reduced , about 1 minute. top pork withe sauce and serve hot.
