in a mixing bowl , cream butter and sugar. add eggs and vanilla. mix well. stir in applesauce. combine the flour , baking soda and spices. stir into creamed mixture. fold in nuts. fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full. bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar if desired. yield: about 2 dozen.
