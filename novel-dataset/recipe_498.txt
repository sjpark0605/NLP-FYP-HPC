in large pot of boiling , salted water , cook the pasta until al dente. drain , reserving 1 cup of the pasta cooking water and the pot. meanwhile , in a small skillet , heat 2 t olive oil over medium heat. add half of the garlic and cook until golden , about 30 seconds. stir in the breadcrumbs and remove from the heat. in the reserved pasta pot , heat the remaining 2 t olive oil over medium heat. add the remaining garlic and cook until golden , about 30 seconds. add the zucchini and cook , turning occasionally , until crisp-tender , about 5 minutes. stir in the corn , cooked pasta and reserved 1 cup pasta cooking water. add 1 cup cheese and toss. season with salt and pepper. stir the remaining cheese and basil into the breadcrumb mixture and sprinkle on top of the pasta.
