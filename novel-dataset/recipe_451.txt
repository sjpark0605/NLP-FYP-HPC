in blender , mix base mix together until no large chunks of banana are left. divide base mix into thirds. put 1 / 3 base mix back into blender with 2 strawberries - mix until smooth and no chunks of strawberry are left. pour into glasses. put 1 / 3 base mix back into blender with oranges - mix until smooth and no chunks are left. slowly pour over the back of a spoon into glass on top of strawberry mix. put 1 / 3 base mix back into blender with pineapple - mix until smooth and little to no chunks are left. slowly pour over the back of a spoon into glass on top of orange mix. sprinkle top with granola.
