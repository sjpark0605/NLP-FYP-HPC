saut seafood and shallots in 3 tablespoons butter and seafood seasoning. cook 5 minutes. sprinkle with 1 / 4 cup chicken broth , salt and pepper. set aside. saut mushrooms in 1 tablespoon of butter. add to seafood. in another pan melt the remaining butter and add flour to make paste. add milk , chicken broth , emeril's essence and tomato paste stir until smooth. combine sauce and seafood mixture and place in a casserole or individual ramekins. bake at 400f degrees for 30 minutes.
