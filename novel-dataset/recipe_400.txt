in a large non-stick skillet fry bacon , remove from skillet and drain on paper towels. when cool enough to handle chop coarsely and set aside. remove all but 1 tbl of bacon grease. heat skillet over medium heat and add the peppers and cook for about 5 to 6 minutes. add the onion and cook until just crisp tender. add the cabbage and wilt until the desired tenderness , about 10 minutes. add the bacon back to the skillet and mix well. add salt and lots of pepper to taste. enjoy !.
