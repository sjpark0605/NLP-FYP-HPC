heat oil in rondo or large braising pan until hot. add mushrooms , shallots , garlic and bell peppers to pan. saute until mushrooms are softened. add rice to pan. cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until rice is hot. stir in soy sauce , green onions , sugar and rice vinegar. cook , stirring occasionally , about 5 minutes. stir in sesame oil and season with salt. spread rice mixture on sheet pan to cool. cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
