preheat oven to 375f. lightly grease muffin cups or spray with cooking spray. place orange pieces in blender and blend until finely chopped. add margarine , eggs , and milk and blend until well mixed. combine flour , sugar , baking powder , and baking soda in a large bowl. make a well in center and add orange mixture , stirring just until moistened. fold in blueberries. fill muffin cups and bake for 22-25 minutes or until golden. cool 10 minutes in pan before removing.
