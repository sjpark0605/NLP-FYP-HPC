mix dry ingredients together , set aside. whisk together the egg yolks , milk , butter and vanilla. add liquid mixture to dry mixture and stir to blend. beat egg whites until stiff. fold stiff egg whites into mixture. carefully fold in coarsely grated apples. make sure batter is fairly liquid , not thick so it spreads out on the griddle. heat griddle to 350f. griddle / pan is ready when water droplets jump around and evaporate. cook on griddle until bubbles appear and edges are dry. flip only once.
