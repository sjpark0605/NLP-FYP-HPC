preheat oven to 350 degrees f. cook yams in boiling water long enough to soften , but still firm. peel and cut into 1 / 2" slices. peel and core apples. slice thinly. in a butter casserole dish , altenate layers of yams , apples and pineapple , dotting with butter , sprinkling with cloves and sugar--until all are used. pour amaretto over all. cover and bake for 30-40 minutes or until apples and sweet potatoes are tender.
