heat a large skillet on medium hi heat for 3 minutes. melt 2 tbsp butter in skillet. season flesh side of fish with 1 1 / 2 tsp old bay seasoning. place fish skin side up in skillet. cook 5 minutes , turning fish after 3 minutes. remove fish from skillet. keep warm. reduce heat to medium. stir wine into skillet , scraping bottom to loosen browned bits. add remaining butter and green onion. cook and stil 1 minute. gradually stir in cream. stir in mustard and remaining old bay. simmer 2 minutes or until slightly thickened. stir in crabmeat. return fish to skillet , cover. cook 3 minutes longer or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
