grease 10" tube pan. cut wax paper to fit bottom of pan , insert , grease and flour. pour sherry on raisins. heat oven to 275. chop walnuts , grate orange and squeeze juice from orange. sift flour with baking powder , salt and nutmeg , save 1 / 4 c of flour for nuts & raisins. beat eggs until thick. cream butter and sugar add in beaten eggs. drain raisins and combine with nuts and the 1 / 4 c flour. add juice of orange & sherry alternately with dry ingredients , combine all with the creamed butter / sugar / egg mixture and stir in vanilla. add floured nuts and raisins last. bake for 2 hours. cool thoroughly before removing from tube pan.
