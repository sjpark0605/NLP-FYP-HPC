preheat oven to 350 degrees. in a medium-sized mixing bowl , blend together the margarine or oil , extract , and sugar. fold in the canned pineapple. stir in the salt and baking powder , then the flours , blending well. do not beat. drop mixture by tablespoonfuls on parchment-lined insulated cookie sheets or greased regular cookie sheets. bake for about 20 minutes or until set. when done , cookies should be only very lightly browned. cool on wire racks.
