set oven to 300 degrees. grease a large baking sheet. in a mixing bowl cream butter , sugar and vanilla until fluffy. gradually add flour and mix well. stir in ground almonds. shape into 1-inch balls. place 2 inches apart on baking sheet / s. bake for about 18-20 minutes , or until bottoms are lightly browned. cool on wire racks. in a small bowl combine icing sugar and nutmeg. gently roll cooled cookies in the sugar mixture.
