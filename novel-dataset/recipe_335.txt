combine all filling ingredients except rhubarb and pecans in large bowl. add rhubarb. toss until well coated. spoon into prepared crust. sprinkle with pecans , if using. set aside. combine 1 cup flour and 2 / 3 cup sugar in medium bowl. cut in 1 / 2 cup butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. sprinkle mixture over rhubarb. cover edge of crust with 2-inch strip aluminum foil. bake for 50 to 60 minutes or until topping is golden brown and filling bubbles around edges. remove aluminum foil during last 10 minutes , if desired.
