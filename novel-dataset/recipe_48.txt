cream butter , gradually add sugar , beating well. add eggs and beat well. combine flour , spices and salt-add to creamed mixture. mix well. combine persimmon pulp and baking soda. stir well and add to creamed mixture. stir in raisins and pecans. drop dough by tablespoons onto greased cookie sheets. bake at 350f for 12 to 15 minutes or until done. usually yields about 3 dozen.
