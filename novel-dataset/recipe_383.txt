heat oven to 350f. spray bottom only of 13x9-inch pan with baking spray with flour. in large bowl , beat cake ingredients with electric mixer on low speed 30 seconds. beat on medium speed 2 minutes. pour into pan. in large bowl , beat cream cheese , sugar and 1 egg on medium speed until smooth and creamy. stir in chocolate chips. spoon filling by tablespoonfuls evenly over top of batter. using table knife , swirl filling through batter. sprinkle with nuts. bake 40 to 45 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. cool completely , about 1 hour. spread frosting over cake. store covered in refrigerator.
