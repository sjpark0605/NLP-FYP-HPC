combine chicken , minced garlic , ground white pepper , soy sauce , salt , oyster sauce , and sugar in a bowl and mix thoroughly. allow to marinate for at least 1 / 2 an hour in the refrigerator. preheat a frying pan over high heat and add peanut oil. heat oil for 2 minutes and place the marinated chicken pieces in the pan. cook for 5-10 minutes. drain left over oil from chicken on paper towels. serve with jasmine rice or sticky rice , lettuce leaves , and cucumber slices.
