1. mix chicken , bacon , ritz cracker crumbs , eggs , worcestershire sauce and pepper until well blended. shape into 4 patties. 2. heat oil in large skillet on medium heat 1 minute. add patties. cover. cook 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through , topping each burger with a slice of cheese for the last minute of the cooking time. 3. place burgers on bottom halves of buns. top each with 1 pickle slice and 1 lettuce leaf. cover with tops of buns.
