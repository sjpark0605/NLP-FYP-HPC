preheat oven to 425 degrees and spray 24 muffin cups with non-stick cooking spray. over medium heat , brown the sausage in a skillet. drain and set aside. in a large bowl , combine the biscuit mix , milk , water , chilies and egg. mix well. stir in the corn and drained sausage. gently mix in grated cheese. fill the prepared muffin tins 2 / 3 full and bake in preheated oven for 15 to 18 minutes. cool 5 minutes. remove from pan and place of a wire rack to cool. serve warm or allow to cool completely , place in a zip-lock bag and freeze. to reheat: defrost overnight in refrigerator , wrap in foil and place in cold oven. set temperature for 350 and allow oven to come to temperature , warm for 8 to 10 minutes or until heated through.
